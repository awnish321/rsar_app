Instructions

  • Tap on download video to open downloaded files chapter wise.
  • Tap on chapter number to download chapter videos.
  • After downloading chapter video tap on(start the scan) button to start scanning. The play icon will appear on the screen.
  • Point the camera to scan the targeted image to view the video.
  • Make sure that your hands(device) do not shake while scanning the image.
  • Tap on the icon button to start the video.
  • Double tap on the camera screen to go to the setting options.
